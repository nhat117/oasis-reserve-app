1. Logging In

Go to your dashboard address in a web browser (for example, admin.estique.com.au).

1
Type in your email and password.
2
Click Sign In.
Screenshot

Login screen

Forgot your password? Click Forgot password? under the Sign In button and follow the steps in the email you receive.

There are two kinds of accounts: Admin and Employee. Admins can see everything, including money settings and staff accounts. Employees see a smaller, simpler version of the dashboard.

2. The Dashboard Layout

Once you're signed in, you'll see a sidebar on the left with the different sections of the dashboard. On a phone, this becomes a menu you can open with the ☰ button.

Screenshot

Full dashboard with sidebar labeled

Your business name is shown at the top of the sidebar. Click the small arrow button to collapse the sidebar and make more room on screen.

3. Stats — Your Business at a Glance

This is the first page you see. It shows quick numbers like how many bookings you've had, and other summary information for your business.

Screenshot

4. Bookings — Managing Appointments

This is where you see every appointment — past, today, and upcoming.

Creating a new booking

1
Click the Tạo lịch (Create Booking) button.
2
Choose the service and the staff member.
3
Pick the date and time.
4
Add the customer's name, phone number, and email.
5
Click Save.
Screenshot

New booking form

Changing or cancelling a booking

Click on any booking in the list to open it. You can change the details, mark it as completed, or cancel it.

5. Customers

Every customer who books or pays is automatically added here — you don't need to type them in yourself. This list shows:

  • Their name and phone number
  • How many times they've visited
  • Their membership tier, if they have one

Use the search box at the top to find a customer by name or phone number.

Screenshot

Customers list

6. Sales (Point of Sale)

This is where you take payment for a service — either for a walk-in customer or someone with a booking.

Taking a payment

1
Click Thanh toán mới (New Sale) at the top of the list, or pick an existing appointment from the Lịch hẹn tab on the left.
2
Choose the service (and any extra add-on services) from the list.
3
If it's a walk-in, type in the customer's name and phone number.
4
Add a discount code if the customer has one.
5
Choose the payment method — cash, card, etc.
6
Click the Charge button to complete the sale.
Screenshot

Point of sale screen with an appointment selected
Every completed sale is saved automatically and shows up later in your Excel report — see section 12.

7. Services Admin

This is your price list — every treatment you offer, with its price and how long it takes.

Adding a new service

1
Click Thêm dịch vụ (Add Service).
2
Type in the name and a short description.
3
Set the price and how many minutes it takes.
4
Upload a photo if you have one.
5
Click Save.
Screenshot

Services list with Add Service form open

To edit or remove a service, click the pencil icon next to it, or the menu (⋯) to delete it.

8. Staff & Time Off Admin

Manage the people who work at your salon here.

Adding a staff member

1
Click Thêm nhân viên (Add Staff).
2
Enter their name, email, and phone number.
3
Set their working hours and any regular break time.
4
Click Save.
Screenshot

Staff list

Marking a staff member as unavailable

Use the section below the staff list to mark a specific day off for one staff member — for example, if they're sick or on holiday.

11. Settings Admin

Settings is a menu of smaller tools. Tap any row to open it.

SettingWhat it does
Shop InfoYour business name, address, and opening hours.
Display & ThemeYour logo and homepage image.
Account ManagementAdd or remove admin/employee logins.
Employee PermissionsChoose which tabs employees are allowed to see.
PaymentsTurn on card payments (Stripe/Square) and set surcharges.
NotificationsTurn on SMS/email reminders sent to customers before their appointment.
Email SettingsConnect the email service used to send messages to customers.
Membership TiersSet up loyalty tiers for repeat customers.
Discount CodesCreate promo codes customers can use at checkout.
Activity LogSee a history of changes made in the dashboard, and download it as a CSV file.
Export Excel ReportDownload your sales and bookings as an Excel file — see below.

12. Exporting a Report for Your Accountant

This creates an Excel file with everything a bookkeeper needs: your sales, appointments, service price list, and staff — all in one file, ready to send.

1
Go to Settings and tap Xuất báo cáo Excel (Export Excel Report).
2
Choose the time period: This Month, Last Month, This Year, or a Custom range.
3
Click Tải xuống Excel (Download Excel).
4
The file downloads to your computer — you can email it straight to your bookkeeper.
Screenshot

Export Excel modal with date range selected

What's inside the file

SheetContents
SummaryTotal revenue, refunds, net revenue, and a breakdown by payment method.
SalesEvery payment taken, with date, customer, amount, and payment method.
AppointmentsEvery booking, with date, time, customer, staff member, and status.
ServicesYour full price list.
StaffYour team, with how many appointments each person has completed.
Screenshot

Opened Excel file showing the Summary sheet
The file only includes the date range you picked — so pick "This Year" if you want everything for a tax return.

13. Getting Help

If something looks wrong or isn't working, contact your system administrator or developer with:

  • What you were trying to do
  • What happened instead
  • A screenshot, if possible
